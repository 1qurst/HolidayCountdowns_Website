Website Redesign

May 1, 2024

Overview of Changes

We're excited to announce a comprehensive redesign of our website! This redesign is part of our ongoing effort to enhance the quality and accessibility of our content.

New Features

  • Improved Navigation: Streamlined menus and a more intuitive user interface make it easier than ever to find what you need.
  • Enhanced Visuals: We've updated our look with new colors, fonts, and a layout that's easier on the eyes and compatible with various devices and screen sizes.
  • Faster Load Times: We've optimized our site's performance to improve page load speeds, making your browsing experience smoother and more enjoyable.

User Experience Enhancements

  • Better organized content categories.
  • Made the background darker so users aren't blinded whenever they visit the website.

Social Icon Bug Fix

We fixed a bug with the "Roblox" social icon not appearing. As the background was changed, it was too dark to see the logo. We have now changed it to a lighter logo so you can see it better.

New Pages Added

We have added a Projects page where you can explore our featured projects. Additionally, we have introduced a Contact page for easier communication.

Thank you for your continued support as we improve our online presence. We welcome your feedback, so please let us know what you think!

Thanks to MrDeveloperGhost for suggesting some of these changes.
